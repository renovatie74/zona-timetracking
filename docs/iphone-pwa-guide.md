# Zona Time Tracker — iPhone Setup Guide

**For:** Employees using the mobile app  
**Device:** iPhone (iOS 16 or newer recommended)

---

## Step 1: Open the App

On your iPhone, open **Safari** (the default browser — not Chrome or Firefox).

Go to:

**https://dev-time.zonaproperties.ae**

> You must use Safari to install the app to your Home Screen.

---

## Step 2: Log In

Enter your **email address** and **password**, then tap **Sign In**.

[Screenshot Placeholder — Login screen]

---

## Step 3: Install as PWA (Add to Home Screen)

Once logged in, tap the **Share** button at the bottom of Safari — it looks like a box with an arrow pointing up ↑.

[Screenshot Placeholder — Safari share button]

Scroll down in the menu that appears and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**.

[Screenshot Placeholder — Add to Home Screen option]

A name will be suggested automatically. You can change it or leave it as **Zona Time**. Tap **Add** in the top right corner.

---

## Step 4: Launch from Home Screen

Go to your iPhone Home Screen. You will see the **Zona Time** icon.

[Screenshot Placeholder — Home Screen icon]

Tap the icon to open the app. It will open in full screen, just like a native app — no browser bar.

> After installing, always launch the app from the Home Screen icon, not from Safari.

---

## Step 5: Using the App

Once inside the app, you will see:

- **Check In** — tap to start a session on a project
- **Check Out** — tap to end your current session
- **My Time** — view your time entries
- **Extras** — submit extra work or own cost items
- **Mileage** — record kilometres driven

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Solution |
|---|---|
| App not loading | Check your internet connection and try again |
| Cannot find "Add to Home Screen" | Make sure you are using Safari, not Chrome or Firefox |
| Login fails | Check your email and password, or use "Forgot password?" |
| GPS not working | When prompted, allow the app to access your location |
| App looks blank after opening | Pull down to refresh, or delete the icon and re-install |
| Session already active | Log out and log back in to reset |

---

*If the problem continues, contact your manager or system administrator.*
